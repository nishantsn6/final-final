Milan

Project Milan aims to empower the farmers of our country by introducing modern farming techniques like Aquaponics and Hydroponics. Project Milan also aims to conserve our natural resources, land and water, and improve health of the citizens by providing organic vegetables at cheap rates.

About Milan

Introducing less water intensive and sustainable forms of farming in the Indian agricultural sector.

Project Milan is a joint venture of Enactus-DTU and Enactus-Southampton. We strive forward to uplift the farmers in India and save our natural resources, land and water.

Problem

Farmers bearing the brunt of crop failures and compounding agricultural loans, leading to countless farmer suicides.

The farmers in India suffer a lot due to their low income high expenditure on farming. Water, our natural resource is getting scarce day by day.

Our Work

Helping farmers expand into aquaculture by setting up aquaponics plants, which allow simultaneous cultivation of fishes and crops.

We are giving good earning opportunities to farmers, saving our natural resources, land and water, and providing citizens with healthy organic vegetables and fishes by implementing advanced farming techniques like Aquaponics.

Future Goals

To change the landscape of farming in India.

To uplift the whole farming community in and near Delhi NCR by introducing them with advanced farming techniques like aquaponics giving them an opportunity to earn well.
